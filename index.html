About JP Prototype

Founded in 2018, JP Prototype is a full-service manufacturing partner specializing in CNC machining, 3D printing, vacuum casting, mold manufacturing, and metal fabrication.

We deliver true end-to-end solutions — from rapid prototyping to mass production — enabling both low-volume development builds and high-volume production programs under one roof.

Our expertise supports demanding industries including medical devices, aerospace, industrial automation, automotive, and consumer electronics.

"From concept validation to production launch, we transform complex designs into reliable, production-ready components — with precision, speed, and accountability."
